## What Is Agency Mode?

Agency Mode transforms A-Stats from a single-team content tool into a multi-client content operation. It gives agencies the infrastructure to manage content programs for multiple clients — each with their own workspace, branding, and access controls — from a single account.

---

## White-Label Client Portals

Each client gets a branded portal where they can view their content, analytics, and reports. The portal carries your agency's branding — logo, colors, and domain — so clients see a professional, cohesive experience without knowing the underlying platform.

Client portals are access-controlled with time-limited tokens. Clients see only their own content and data.

---

## Client Management

The agency dashboard provides a centralized view of all your clients:

- **Add clients** with their own project workspace, brand voice settings, and integrations.
- **Track content production** across all clients from a single overview.
- **Generate branded reports** showing content performance, SEO scores, and publishing activity for each client.
- **Control access** — decide which team members work on which client accounts.

---

## How It Fits Into Your Workflow

Agency Mode builds on top of the same project system that individual users rely on. Each client is a project with its own:

| Feature | Scope |
|---|---|
| **Brand Voice** | Per-client writing style and tone |
| **Knowledge Vault** | Client-specific documents and context |
| **Integrations** | Client's WordPress, GSC, social accounts |
| **Team Access** | Assign specific team members to each client |
| **Analytics** | Client-level performance data |

This means your team uses the same content creation, SEO scoring, and scheduling tools — just organized by client rather than by a single brand.

---

## Who Agency Mode Is For

**Content agencies** managing editorial calendars for multiple clients who need clear workspace separation and branded deliverables.

**SEO agencies** producing optimized content at scale who need per-client keyword tracking, scoring, and reporting.

**Marketing consultancies** offering content as a service who want to present a white-labeled experience to their clients.

> Agency Mode is available on the Professional plan and above. The Agency plan includes unlimited client workspaces and full white-label branding.
