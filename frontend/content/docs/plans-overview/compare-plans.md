### Free

Get started at no cost. The Free plan includes 3 AI-generated articles per month and 1 project with Brand Voice configuration. It is a good way to evaluate the platform before committing to a paid tier.

### Starter -- $29/month

Built for individual creators and small sites. You get 15 articles per month across 3 projects, plus the full suite of content tools: SEO and AEO scoring, content calendar, social scheduling, Knowledge Vault, AI images, WordPress integration, and Google Search Console data.

### Professional -- $79/month

Designed for growing teams and freelancers managing multiple clients. Includes 50 articles per month, 10 projects, everything in Starter, plus agency mode and priority support.

### Agency -- $199/month

For agencies that need no limits. Unlimited articles, unlimited projects, white-label reporting, full API access, and dedicated support. Everything the platform offers, without caps.

### Money-Back Guarantee

All paid plans come with a 14-day money-back guarantee. If the platform does not meet your needs, request a full refund within 14 days of purchase -- no questions asked.

### Switching Plans

You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your account billing page. Upgrades take effect immediately. When you downgrade, your current plan remains active until the end of the billing period.

### Need Help Choosing?

If you are unsure which plan fits your workflow, start with Free to explore the interface and content quality. You can upgrade to any paid plan when you are ready to scale.
