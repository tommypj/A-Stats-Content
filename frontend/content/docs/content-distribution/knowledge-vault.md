## What Is the Knowledge Vault?

The Knowledge Vault is your private document library inside A-Stats. Upload your brand guidelines, product documentation, research reports, competitor analyses, or any reference material — and the AI uses it as context when generating content.

The result: AI-generated articles that reflect your specific expertise, terminology, and perspective rather than generic internet content.

---

## How It Works

When you upload a document to the Knowledge Vault, A-Stats processes it into searchable chunks and stores them in a vector database. During article generation, the AI retrieves the most relevant passages from your uploaded documents and weaves them into the content it produces.

This is the same retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) approach used by enterprise AI systems — applied to your content workflow.

---

## What You Can Upload

The Knowledge Vault accepts a range of document formats:

| Format | Use Case |
|---|---|
| **PDF** | Brand guidelines, research reports, whitepapers |
| **Text files** | Style guides, product specs, SOPs |
| **Documents** | Internal knowledge bases, training materials |

Each document is automatically indexed and made available to the AI across all articles in that project.

---

## Why It Matters

Without the Knowledge Vault, AI-generated content relies on the model's general training data. It produces competent writing, but it cannot know your product names, internal terminology, competitive positioning, or proprietary insights.

With it, every article is grounded in your actual knowledge. A technology company can upload its product documentation so the AI accurately describes features. A consultancy can upload its methodology framework so articles reflect its unique approach.

---

## Query Your Knowledge

Beyond article generation, the Knowledge Vault also supports direct querying. Ask a question about your uploaded documents and get an AI-generated answer drawn from your own sources — useful for quickly finding information across a large document library.

> The Knowledge Vault is available on all paid plans. Each plan includes a generous document upload limit, with higher tiers supporting larger libraries.
