## AI Image Generation

Stock photos are generic. Custom design work is expensive and slow. A-Stats bridges the gap with AI-powered image generation built directly into your content workflow -- producing original visuals that match your article's subject matter without leaving the platform.

## Three Prompts Per Article

When A-Stats generates an article, the content pipeline also produces three distinct image prompts tailored to different sections or themes within the piece. Each prompt targets a different visual concept, so you are not choosing between three variations of the same idea. You get three genuinely different images to work with.

Review the prompts, adjust the wording if you want a different direction, and generate images with a single click. The result is a set of original visuals created specifically for your content, not pulled from a library that your competitors are also using.

## Inline Preview and Selection

Generated images appear as inline previews alongside your article content. You can evaluate each one in context -- seeing how it will look within the article layout -- before committing to a selection. If none of the initial results are right, regenerate from any of the three prompts with a modified description.

## Project Media Library

Every generated image is automatically stored in your project's media library. Images are organized by project and linked to their source article, making them easy to find and reuse. Need a hero image for a social post promoting an older article? It is already in your library, tagged and searchable.

The media library serves as a growing visual asset collection that becomes more valuable over time as your content library expands.

## On-Brand Visuals at Scale

For teams publishing multiple articles per week, sourcing unique images for each piece is a real bottleneck. AI generation removes that bottleneck entirely. Combined with A-Stats' brand voice settings, your generated images align with the tone and subject matter your audience expects.

> **Tip:** Use your strongest generated images as featured images for social media posts. The same media library that stores article images is accessible from the social scheduling composer.

## Availability

AI image generation is available on all paid plans. The number of generations per month scales with your plan tier. Generated images are stored permanently in your project media library regardless of plan changes.
