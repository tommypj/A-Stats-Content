## Original Images Without the Bottleneck

Stock photos are generic. Custom design work is expensive and slow. A-Stats generates original visuals tailored to your article's content — directly from the editor, no separate tools needed.

---

## What You Get

When A-Stats generates an article, it also produces three distinct image prompts — each targeting a different visual concept from the piece. You're not choosing between three variations of the same idea; you get three genuinely different images to work with.

Review the prompts, adjust the wording for a different direction, and generate with a single click. The result is a set of original visuals created specifically for your content.

---

## Built Into Your Workflow

- **Inline previews** — see generated images in context alongside your article before committing
- **One-click regeneration** — modify a prompt and regenerate if the first result isn't right
- **Project media library** — every generated image is automatically stored, organized by project, and linked to its source article
- **Reusable across content** — need a hero image for a social post promoting an older article? It's already in your library

## Scale Without Sourcing Headaches

For teams publishing multiple articles per week, finding unique images for each piece is a real bottleneck. AI generation removes it entirely. Your media library grows into a searchable visual asset collection that becomes more valuable over time.
