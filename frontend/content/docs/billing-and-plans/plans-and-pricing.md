## Plan Overview

The platform offers four subscription tiers designed to grow with your content production needs. All plans include the core content generation features; higher tiers expand generation volume, team size, storage, and access to advanced features.

## Free Plan

The Free plan is available to anyone who creates an account. No credit card is required.

**Monthly generation limit:** 10 generations

**What is included:**
- AI article and outline generation (10/month combined)
- SEO scoring and analysis
- Basic analytics dashboard
- 1 project
- Image library with 500 MB storage
- Knowledge Vault (up to 3 sources)
- 1 team member (owner only)

**What is not included:**
- Agency mode
- White-label branding
- Custom integrations (WordPress, GSC, social media)
- Priority support

The Free plan is suited for individuals exploring the platform or publishing content infrequently. For regular publishing workflows, the Starter plan provides a meaningful step up.

## Starter Plan

**Monthly generation limit:** 50 generations

**What is included, beyond Free:**
- 50 AI generations per month
- Up to 3 projects
- WordPress publishing integration
- Google Search Console connection
- Social media scheduling (2 accounts per project)
- Image storage: 5 GB
- Knowledge Vault: up to 20 sources
- Up to 3 team members per project
- Email support

The Starter plan is designed for solo content creators and small teams publishing consistently — several articles per week across one or a few websites.

## Pro Plan

**Monthly generation limit:** 150 generations

**What is included, beyond Starter:**
- 150 AI generations per month
- Unlimited projects
- Social media scheduling (unlimited accounts per project)
- Image storage: 20 GB
- Knowledge Vault: unlimited sources
- Up to 10 team members per project
- Agency mode (client management, white-label branding, client portal)
- Content decay detection and alerts
- AEO tracking and scoring
- Bulk content generation workflows
- Priority email support

The Pro plan is the primary choice for content agencies and in-house marketing teams that produce high content volume across multiple clients or brands.

## Enterprise Plan

**Monthly generation limit:** 300 generations

**What is included, beyond Pro:**
- 300 AI generations per month
- Unlimited team members per project
- Custom portal subdomain for agency mode
- Dedicated support with faster response times
- Advanced usage reporting
- Custom onboarding assistance (arranged at signup)

Enterprise is suited for large agencies, publishing operations, and enterprise marketing teams that require maximum generation volume and dedicated support.

## Annual Billing Discount

All paid plans are available on monthly or annual billing:

- **Monthly billing** — Pay month-to-month, cancel anytime with no penalty.
- **Annual billing** — Pay for 12 months upfront at a **20% discount** compared to monthly pricing.

Annual billing is recommended for established workflows where you know the platform will be in regular use. The discount is applied automatically when you select annual billing at checkout.

> **Tip:** If you are starting with a new workflow, consider monthly billing for the first 1–2 months to validate your generation volume needs, then switch to annual billing once you know which plan fits.
