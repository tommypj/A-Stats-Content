## Billing via LemonSqueezy

All subscription billing is handled by LemonSqueezy, our third-party payment processor. Your credit card details are stored securely by LemonSqueezy — the platform itself never stores card numbers. You can manage payment methods, view invoices, and update billing information directly in the LemonSqueezy billing portal.

## Accessing the Billing Portal

1. Navigate to **Settings > Billing** in the platform.
2. Click **Manage Subscription** or **Open Billing Portal**.
3. You are redirected to the LemonSqueezy billing portal in a new tab.

From the billing portal you can:
- View current plan and next billing date
- Update credit card or payment method
- Download past invoices as PDFs
- Change to annual or monthly billing
- Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription

## Upgrading Your Plan

### From the Platform
1. Go to **Settings > Billing**.
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**.
3. Select the target plan.
4. Review the pricing and confirm.
5. The upgrade takes effect immediately.

### What Happens at Upgrade
- You gain access to the higher plan's features and generation limit immediately.
- Billing is prorated: you are charged for the remaining days of the current billing period at the new plan's rate, minus a credit for the unused days of your current plan.
- Your next full billing cycle is charged at the new plan's rate.

> **Tip:** Upgrading mid-month means your first charge will be a partial amount. Your second charge will be the full new plan rate. This is normal.

## Downgrading Your Plan

### From the Billing Portal
1. Open the billing portal via **Settings > Billing > Manage Subscription**.
2. Select your current plan.
3. Choose a lower tier.
4. Confirm the downgrade.

### What Happens at Downgrade
- The downgrade takes effect at the **end of your current billing period**, not immediately. You continue to have access to your current plan's features until the period ends.
- On the next billing date, your account moves to the new plan and new limits apply.

### Impact on Existing Content
Downgrading does not delete any existing content. However, if you downgrade and your current data exceeds the new plan's limits:

| Limit Exceeded | What Happens |
|---------------|-------------|
| Projects exceed plan limit | Existing projects are preserved but you cannot create new ones until you are within limit |
| Team members exceed limit | Existing members retain access; you cannot add new members until within limit |
| Storage exceeds limit | Existing images are preserved; new uploads and generation are blocked |
| Knowledge sources exceed limit | Existing sources remain active; new uploads are blocked |

No data is deleted automatically as a result of downgrading. You are given time to manually reduce usage to the new plan's limits.

> **Tip:** Review your usage before downgrading. If you have 8 projects and are moving to a 3-project plan, decide which projects to archive before the downgrade date.

## Canceling Your Subscription

1. Open the billing portal via **Settings > Billing > Manage Subscription**.
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**.
3. Select a reason (optional) and confirm.

### What Happens at Cancellation
- Your subscription is marked as canceled, but access continues until the **end of the current billing period**.
- At the end of the billing period, your account moves to the **Free plan** with Free plan limits.
- All your content, projects, and data are preserved; you are not locked out of the platform.
- No further charges occur after cancellation.

There are no cancellation fees or long-term contracts. Monthly billing can be canceled at any time. Annual billing can be canceled to stop future renewal — you retain access for the remainder of the paid year.

## Reactivating After Cancellation

If you cancel and later want to resubscribe:

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**.
2. Click **Reactivate** or **Choose a Plan**.
3. Select your plan and complete payment.

Reactivation restores your previous plan limits immediately. All previously created content (from when you had a higher plan) becomes accessible again.

## Billing Issues and Failed Payments

If a payment fails (expired card, insufficient funds, etc.):
- LemonSqueezy automatically retries the payment over the next several days.
- You receive an email notification of the failed payment with a link to update your payment method.
- Access to paid features continues during the retry window.
- If payment is not resolved after all retry attempts, the account reverts to the Free plan.

To resolve a payment issue, update your payment method in the billing portal as soon as you receive the failure notification.

## Invoices and Tax

Invoices for each billing period are available in the LemonSqueezy billing portal in PDF format. Invoices include line items, total amount, and any applicable taxes. LemonSqueezy handles tax calculation and remittance for applicable jurisdictions. If you need invoices addressed to a company name or require a VAT number on invoices, update your billing details in the LemonSqueezy portal.
