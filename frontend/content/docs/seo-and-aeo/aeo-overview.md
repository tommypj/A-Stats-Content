## Get Cited by AI Search

When someone asks a question today, the answer increasingly comes from an AI-generated summary — ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, Bing Copilot. The content these engines choose to cite determines which brands get visibility in this new landscape.

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring your content so AI systems are more likely to select it as a source. A-Stats scores content for AEO alongside traditional SEO, in a single editor.

---

## Why AEO Matters Now

- **Google AI Overviews** appear above organic results, often reducing clicks to underlying pages
- **ChatGPT and Perplexity** cite web sources directly in their responses, sending traffic to referenced pages
- **Bing Copilot** synthesizes answers and attributes sources inline

Content that isn't optimized for these systems risks becoming invisible — even if it ranks well in traditional search.

---

## What AI Engines Look For

| Quality | Why It Gets Cited |
|---|---|
| **Direct Answer Clarity** | Concise answers near the top of a section are easier to extract |
| **Structural Clarity** | Clean headings, numbered lists, and definition patterns are easier for AI to parse |
| **FAQ Sections** | Question-and-answer formatting maps directly to AI query patterns |
| **Factual Density** | Specific data, statistics, and verifiable claims are preferred over vague commentary |
| **Topical Authority** | Comprehensive single-topic coverage signals reliability |

These aren't the same signals that drive traditional search ranking. A page can rank first on Google yet never be cited by an AI overview.

> **Tip:** The fastest way to improve an AEO score is to ensure every major section begins with a clear, direct answer to the question implied by its heading. AI engines extract these lead-in sentences disproportionately often.

## The Early Mover Advantage

Most content teams aren't thinking about AEO yet. Those that are have a measurable edge: their content appears in AI-generated answers while competitors remain limited to traditional search. As AI search adoption accelerates, that advantage compounds.
