## SEO Scoring Overview

Most SEO tools evaluate your content after it has already been published. By that point, you are reacting to problems rather than preventing them. A-Stats takes a different approach: a 10-point SEO scoring system that evaluates your content in real time, as you write.

### How It Works

Every article in A-Stats is scored continuously against ten ranking factors. As you edit a sentence, add a heading, or adjust your keyword usage, the score updates instantly. There is no waiting for a crawl, no switching between tabs, and no guesswork about whether your changes improved things.

This is not a checklist you review once before hitting publish. It is a live feedback loop built into the editor itself.

### The 10 Scoring Factors

| Factor | What It Measures |
|---|---|
| **Keyword Density** | Whether your primary keyword appears at the right frequency --- not too sparse, not stuffed |
| **Title Optimization** | Keyword placement, length, and clarity of your page title |
| **Meta Description** | Presence, length, and keyword inclusion in your meta description |
| **Heading Structure** | Proper use of H1 through H4 headings with logical hierarchy |
| **Internal Linking** | Number and relevance of links to other pages on your site |
| **Readability** | Sentence complexity, paragraph length, and overall reading ease |
| **Content Length** | Whether the article meets competitive depth for its target keyword |
| **Image Optimization** | Alt text presence and descriptive quality for all images |
| **URL Structure** | Clean, keyword-relevant slug formatting |
| **Content Freshness** | Signals that indicate the content is current and up to date |

Each factor contributes to a composite score displayed alongside your editor. Individual factor breakdowns show exactly where to focus your effort.

### Why Real-Time Scoring Matters

Traditional SEO audit tools work on a publish-then-fix cycle. You write content, publish it, run an audit, find issues, go back to the CMS, make changes, republish, and audit again. That loop can take hours or days.

With A-Stats, the audit happens while you type. If your keyword density drops below the optimal range mid-draft, you see it immediately. If your heading structure breaks hierarchy, the score reflects it before you finish the paragraph. The result is content that ships optimized on the first publish, not the third revision.

### How Scores Are Calculated

Each of the ten factors is scored individually, and the composite score is a weighted aggregate. Factors that carry more ranking weight in modern search algorithms --- such as content depth, readability, and heading structure --- are weighted more heavily than purely technical checks.

Scores are recalculated on every keystroke. There is no delay, no manual refresh, and no "re-analyze" button. The number you see is always current.

### Built for Writers, Not Just Technicians

The scoring interface is designed to be useful without requiring deep SEO expertise. Each factor includes a plain-language explanation of what it measures and why it matters. A content writer with no technical background can use the score to produce well-optimized articles without consulting an SEO specialist.

> **Tip:** Use the per-factor breakdown to prioritize improvements. Raising a low-scoring factor by even one or two points often has more impact than perfecting a factor that is already scoring well.

### Part of a Larger Pipeline

SEO scoring in A-Stats does not exist in isolation. It integrates directly with the AI content generation pipeline, the AEO scoring system, and one-click WordPress publishing. Content generated by the AI pipeline is pre-optimized against these same ten factors, giving you a strong starting score that you can then refine.

Every article you publish through A-Stats carries its SEO score in the dashboard, making it easy to track optimization quality across your entire content library over time.
