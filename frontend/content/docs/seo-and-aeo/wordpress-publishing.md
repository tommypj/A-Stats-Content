## One-Click Publishing to WordPress

A-Stats connects directly to your WordPress site, letting you publish finished articles with a single click. Everything goes together — content, title, excerpt, featured image, SEO fields, categories, and tags — so there's no manual re-entry on the WordPress side.

---

## What Gets Published

| Field | Details |
|---|---|
| **Article Content** | Full HTML with heading structure, images, and formatting |
| **Title** | As configured in A-Stats |
| **Excerpt** | Auto-generated or manually written summary |
| **Featured Image** | AI-generated or selected image, uploaded to your WordPress media library |
| **SEO Title & Description** | Optimized metadata pushed to your SEO plugin |
| **Categories & Tags** | Mapped to existing WordPress taxonomy or created if new |

Everything in a single push. No partial drafts, no copy-pasting between systems.

---

## Part of Your Content Workflow

WordPress publishing is the final step — not a separate process. Write or generate an article, optimize it with live scoring, generate images, review everything in one place, then publish. The article arrives on WordPress fully formed.

Pair it with the Content Calendar to schedule articles for future publication. Content pushes to WordPress at the scheduled time with all metadata intact — hands-off editorial workflows.

---

## Multi-Site Ready

Agencies and media companies managing multiple WordPress sites can connect each one independently. Articles are directed to the appropriate site at publish time, making A-Stats a centralized content hub for distributed publishing.

---

## No Lock-In

Content published to WordPress is standard WordPress content. If you ever stop using A-Stats, your articles, images, categories, and tags remain exactly as published. No proprietary formatting, no shortcodes, no dependencies.
