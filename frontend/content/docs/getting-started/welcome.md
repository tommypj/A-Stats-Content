## What is A-Stats?

A-Stats is a content platform that brings article creation, SEO optimization, analytics, and distribution into one workspace. Instead of juggling separate tools for writing, keyword tracking, social posting, and performance monitoring, everything lives in a single dashboard.

---

## What You Can Do

### Create Articles from a Keyword
Enter a keyword and get a structured, SEO-optimized article in minutes. You control the outline before anything is written, so the result matches your editorial intent — not a generic AI dump.

### Score for SEO and AI Search
Every article gets a real-time SEO score across ten factors and an AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) score that measures how likely AI search engines are to cite your content. Both update as you edit.

### Track Search Performance
Connect Google Search Console to see clicks, impressions, rankings, and trends for every article — directly alongside your content. Automated alerts flag pages that are losing visibility before the decline becomes serious.

### Publish and Distribute
Push articles to WordPress with metadata and featured images in one click. Schedule social posts to LinkedIn, X, and Facebook from the same workspace.

### Upload Brand Knowledge
Feed the AI your brand guidelines, product docs, and reference material so generated content reflects your actual expertise — not generic web knowledge.

### Collaborate with Your Team
Organize work into projects with role-based access. Agencies get white-label client portals and per-client workspaces.

## Who A-Stats Is For

**Content creators and bloggers** who want publish-ready articles faster without sacrificing quality.

**SEO professionals** who need content production and performance monitoring in one place.

**Content teams** who need shared workflows with clear editorial roles.

**Agencies** managing content for multiple clients with separate branding and access controls.

## What Makes A-Stats Different

- **AEO built in**: Score content for AI citation potential alongside traditional SEO — most tools ignore this entirely.
- **Live scoring**: SEO and AEO scores update as you type, not after you publish.
- **Knowledge-grounded AI**: Uploaded brand documents make generated content specific to your business.
- **One workspace**: Outline, write, score, generate images, schedule social posts, and track performance without switching tools.
