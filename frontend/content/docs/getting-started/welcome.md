## What is A-Stats?

A-Stats is an all-in-one content intelligence platform built for SEO professionals, content teams, and agencies who need more than a writing tool. It combines AI-powered article generation, real-time SEO scoring, Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) tracking, Google Search Console analytics, social media scheduling, and team collaboration — all in a single workspace.

Whether you are a solo content creator publishing three articles a week or an agency managing content for twenty clients, A-Stats gives you the infrastructure to produce, optimize, and distribute content at scale without stitching together a dozen separate tools.

---

## Key Capabilities

### AI Article Generation
Go from keyword to published-ready article in minutes. A-Stats uses Claude AI to help you build structured outlines with custom sections, then expands each section into a full article. You control the writing style, voice, and tone at the project level — so every article sounds like it came from the same author.

### SEO Scoring
Every article is scored in real time across ten SEO dimensions: keyword density, title optimization, meta description, heading structure, internal linking opportunities, readability, content length, and more. The score updates as you edit, so you can see the impact of every change before you publish.

### Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)
AEO is what separates A-Stats from traditional SEO tools. As AI-powered search (Google's AI Overviews, Perplexity, ChatGPT) becomes a primary discovery channel, ranking in those surfaces requires a different kind of optimization. A-Stats scores your content for AEO — checking whether it directly answers questions, uses structured formats, and has the kind of clear authority signals that AI systems cite.

### Google Search Console Analytics
Connect your GSC account to pull real search performance data directly into A-Stats. See which articles are gaining or losing impressions, detect content decay before it hurts your rankings, and get alerts when a page drops below your performance threshold.

### Social Media Scheduling
Draft, schedule, and publish to LinkedIn, X (Twitter), and Facebook from inside A-Stats. Social posts can be generated from your article content, keeping your distribution strategy consistent with your editorial calendar.

### Knowledge Vault
Upload your own documents — brand guidelines, research reports, product specs, competitor analyses — and A-Stats makes them available as context when generating content. Your AI-generated articles will reflect your specific knowledge, not generic internet content.

### Image Generation
Generate on-brand images for your articles and social posts directly from the editor. Images are stored in your project's media library and can be reused across content.

### Projects and Teams
Organize your work into projects. Each project has its own brand voice settings, integrations, and team members. Roles (owner, admin, editor, viewer) let you control who can create, edit, or only read content. Agencies get an additional white-label portal for client access.

## What Makes A-Stats Different

Most content tools are built around either writing or analytics — rarely both. A-Stats was designed from the start to close that gap:

- **AEO-first**: Most SEO tools treat AI search as an afterthought. A-Stats scores your content for AI citation potential alongside traditional SEO factors.
- **Feedback at edit time**: SEO and AEO scores update as you type. You do not need to run a separate audit after writing.
- **Workflow in one place**: Outline creation, article writing, SEO review, image generation, and social scheduling are all connected steps, not separate tools with separate exports.
- **Knowledge-grounded AI**: The Knowledge Vault means your AI content reflects your actual expertise and brand context, not generic outputs.
- **Agency-ready**: White-label portals, multi-project workspaces, and role-based access make A-Stats practical for agencies managing multiple clients.

## How A-Stats Handles Your Data

Your content, Knowledge Vault documents, and GSC data are scoped to your account and projects. Team members only see the projects they are invited to. Agency client portals are access-controlled by time-limited tokens. AI generation uses your inputs to construct prompts — your content is not used to train any models.
