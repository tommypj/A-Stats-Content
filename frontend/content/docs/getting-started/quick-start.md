## Get Up and Running

A-Stats is designed so you can go from sign-up to a scored article draft in under ten minutes. Here is what the first session looks like.

---

## Sign Up

Create a free account — no credit card required. The free tier includes enough to explore the full workflow: article generation, SEO scoring, and the editor.

---

## Create a Project

Projects are how A-Stats organizes your work. Each project is a self-contained workspace with its own articles, brand voice settings, integrations, and team members.

If you manage one website, create one project. If you manage multiple sites or clients, create one per site. You can always add more later.

---

## Generate Your First Article

Enter a target keyword and A-Stats produces a structured outline. Review and adjust the outline — add sections, remove irrelevant ones, reorder the flow — then generate the full article. The result is a complete draft with heading structure, natural prose, and your keyword woven in.

---

## Check Your Scores

The editor shows two scores in real time:

- **SEO Score** — ten checks covering keyword usage, title optimization, heading structure, readability, and more.
- **AEO Score** — measures how well your content is structured for AI search citation.

Both update as you edit, so you can see the impact of every change immediately.

---

## What Comes Next

Once you have a scored draft, you can:

- **Edit and polish** directly in the rich-text editor
- **Publish to WordPress** with metadata and featured images in one click
- **Schedule social posts** to promote your article on LinkedIn, X, and Facebook
- **Connect Google Search Console** to track how your articles perform in search
- **Upload brand documents** to the Knowledge Vault so future articles reflect your expertise
- **Invite team members** with role-based access to your project
