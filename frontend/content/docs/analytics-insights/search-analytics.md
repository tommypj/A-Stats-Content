## Your Search Performance in One Place

A-Stats pulls your Google Search Console data directly into your dashboard — clicks, impressions, CTR, and average position for every keyword and page. No CSV exports, no switching between tools.

---

## What You See

| Metric | What It Tells You |
|---|---|
| **Clicks** | How many users clicked through from search results |
| **Impressions** | How often your page appeared, whether clicked or not |
| **CTR** | Click-through rate — the ratio of clicks to impressions |
| **Average Position** | Your page's mean ranking for a given query over the selected period |

Available at both the keyword level and the page level, so you can zoom from a site-wide view down to a single URL.

---

## Find Opportunities

- **High impressions, low CTR** — your page appears in search but doesn't get clicked. Title tags or meta descriptions likely need attention.
- **Position close to page one** — keywords ranking 11-20 are within striking distance. A content refresh could push them onto page one.
- **Date range comparisons** — see which keywords are gaining or losing ground week over week or month over month.

---

## Track Trends Over Time

A-Stats records ranking data daily, building historical trend lines for every keyword and page. Position tracking charts show movement over weeks and months so you can correlate changes with content updates, link building, or algorithm shifts.

Combined with content decay detection, this gives you an early warning system before traffic loss becomes significant.

---

## Why It Matters

By centralizing search performance alongside your editorial calendar and SEO scores, A-Stats eliminates the context-switching that slows down content teams. You see what's working, what's slipping, and where to invest — all from one screen.
