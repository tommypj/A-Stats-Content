## From Keyword to Article in Minutes

A-Stats turns a single keyword into a publish-ready, SEO-optimized article. Rather than generating text in one shot and hoping for the best, the platform uses a structured approach: research the topic, build an outline you can edit, then write the full article based on your approved structure.

---

## What You Get

- **Research-backed content** — every article starts with analysis of what's currently ranking for your keyword, what questions searchers ask, and where content gaps exist.
- **Editorial control** — you review and shape the outline before any prose is written. Add sections, remove irrelevant ones, adjust the angle.
- **Consistent brand voice** — configure tone, style, and vocabulary at the project level, and every article follows those guidelines automatically.
- **Knowledge-grounded output** — upload your brand documents, product specs, or research to the Knowledge Vault, and the AI weaves your specific expertise into every piece.
- **SEO and AEO scoring** — articles are scored in real time for both traditional search optimization and AI search citation potential.
- **AI image prompts** — three distinct image concepts are generated per article, ready for one-click image creation.

---

## Why the Outline Step Matters

Most AI writing tools produce a finished article from a prompt. If the structure is wrong, you're rewriting from scratch.

A-Stats separates structure from writing. You see the proposed sections, headings, and angle before the AI writes a single paragraph. Adjusting an outline takes seconds. Rewriting a 2,000-word article takes much longer. This front-loaded editorial control is what makes the output consistently usable.

---

## Built for Speed and Scale

The full workflow — research, outline, and article — typically completes in under two minutes. The same process powers individual articles and bulk generation of hundreds of pieces, with templates ensuring consistency across large content programs.

> **Tip:** Invest time in your Knowledge Vault uploads and brand voice settings early. The more context the AI has about your brand, the less editing you'll need on generated articles.
