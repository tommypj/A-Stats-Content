## Scale Content Production

Producing one article at a time works for small operations. When you need dozens or hundreds of pieces per month, A-Stats provides bulk generation and a content calendar that turn content production from a serial, manual process into a parallel, managed one.

---

## Bulk Content Generation

Queue multiple articles for production in a single workflow:

- **Templates** — define reusable configurations with preset AI settings, tone, Knowledge Vault sources, and section structures. Apply a template to a batch of keywords and every article meets the same standard. Ideal for product pages, location-based content, or any repeatable content type.
- **CSV import** — upload a spreadsheet of keywords and metadata to generate articles in batch. Each row becomes an article in the queue.
- **Outline review gates** — bulk generation doesn't skip editorial control. You review and approve outlines in batch before article writing begins.

Every article in a batch goes through the same full workflow as an individual piece — research, outline, and article generation — so volume doesn't mean lower quality.

---

## The Content Calendar

A visual timeline of your entire content operation. Articles move through stages — draft, in review, scheduled, published — and appear on the calendar by target publication date.

- **Drag-and-drop scheduling** — move articles between dates in seconds
- **Auto-publish to WordPress** — scheduled articles push to your site automatically with content, featured image, and SEO metadata
- **Progress tracking** — see at a glance which articles are generating, awaiting review, or ready for publication
- **Multi-project views** — aggregate content across all your projects or client sites

---

## Better Together

The real power is combining both systems: plan your month on the calendar, generate all the articles via bulk workflows, review outlines in batch, then let auto-publish handle the rest. A content team can plan a month of articles in an afternoon and have them publish on schedule.

---

## Quality at Scale

- **Template-enforced structure** ensures consistency across large content sets
- **SEO and AEO scoring** on every generated article makes it easy to flag pieces that need attention
- **Knowledge Vault context** keeps terminology and messaging on-brand regardless of volume
- **Role-based access** lets editors review outlines, admins approve schedules, and clients monitor progress

> **Tip:** Start with a small batch of 5-10 articles to calibrate your template settings. Once the output consistently meets your standards, scale up with confidence.
