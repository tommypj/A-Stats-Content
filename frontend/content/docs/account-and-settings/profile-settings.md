## Accessing Profile Settings

Your profile settings are in **Settings > Profile**. This page controls your personal account details — the information associated with your account across all projects and platforms.

## Display Name

Your display name appears in:
- The platform header when you are logged in
- Team member lists in projects you belong to
- Activity logs and audit trails
- Content attribution (articles created or last edited by you)

To change your display name:
1. Open **Settings > Profile**.
2. Click the name field and update the text.
3. Click **Save Changes**.

The change takes effect immediately across the platform.

## Profile Avatar

Your avatar is the profile image shown next to your name in team member lists and in the platform header.

To upload or change your avatar:
1. Open **Settings > Profile**.
2. Click the avatar image (or the camera icon overlay on it).
3. Select an image file from your computer.
   - Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP
   - Recommended size: at least 200 x 200 pixels (square)
   - Maximum file size: 2 MB
4. The image is uploaded and displayed immediately.

If you do not upload a custom avatar, your initials are displayed in a colored circle as a default avatar.

## Email Address

Your email address is used for:
- Logging in to the platform
- Receiving system notifications (billing alerts, team invitations, content alerts)
- Identity verification

Your email address is displayed as read-only on the profile settings page. It cannot be changed inline.

### Changing Your Email Address

To change your email to a new address, use the **Change Email** flow:

1. On **Settings > Profile**, click **Change Email** (shown near the read-only email field).
2. Enter your new email address in the input that appears.
3. Enter your current password to confirm the change.
4. Click **Send Verification Email**.

A verification email is sent to the **new address** you entered. Open that email and click the verification link within **1 hour**. The link expires after 1 hour for security.

5. Once you click the link, your email address is updated to the new one.
6. You will be asked to log in again with the new email address.

> **Tip:** Check your spam folder if the verification email does not arrive within a few minutes. The email is sent from a platform notifications address, which some spam filters flag on first contact.

### If Your Verification Email Expires

If 1 hour passes without clicking the link, the verification token expires. To try again:

1. Return to **Settings > Profile**.
2. Click **Change Email** again.
3. Re-enter the new email address and current password.
4. A new verification email is sent.

### Important Notes About Email Changes
- Your login email changes as soon as you verify the new address. Use the new email to log in going forward.
- Pending team invitations sent to the old email address remain valid. If you were invited to a project using your old address, those invitations are not automatically transferred.
- Billing receipts from LemonSqueezy continue to go to the email on file with LemonSqueezy. Update it separately in the billing portal if needed.

## Timezone and Language

### Timezone
Select your timezone from the dropdown in profile settings. Timezone affects:
- Timestamps displayed for article creation dates, scheduling times, and analytics data
- The "scheduled for" time shown when scheduling social media posts

The platform stores all times in UTC internally; your timezone setting converts those times for display only.

### Language
Select your preferred interface language from the available options. The interface language affects navigation labels, button text, and system messages. It does not affect AI-generated content — content language is controlled per article at generation time.

## Saving Changes

Changes to display name, avatar, timezone, and language are saved by clicking the **Save Changes** button at the bottom of the profile settings form. There is no auto-save on this page. If you navigate away without saving, changes are discarded.

Email changes follow their own separate flow described above and are not part of the main Save Changes button.
